## astrosurface for planetary stacking

1. Load file
2. Click Register
3. IN Analyze and register menu:
    a. Target panel, click detect. A green box appears over the planet.
    b. Quality estimator panel, click analyze. Quality summary box appears, choose fraction of quality ordered images to stacked.
    c. Stacking method panel. Choose number of points.
    d. Stacking panel. Click stack.
4. Preview of stacked image opens. Click edit, dialog asks if you want to close the .ser video. Click yes.
5. Image enhancing through adjust menu
    a. Local_Adj has global sharpening tools.
    b. Wavelets for wavelets
6. White balance
7. HDR - adjust range to hide haloing
8. Saturation - color saturation
